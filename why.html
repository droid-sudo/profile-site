Motivation

My motivation is to help individuals or couples who are seriously looking for a respectful and clearly communicated known donor arrangement.

I value responsibility, health, education, privacy, and clear boundaries. I prefer a thoughtful process where expectations, screening, communication, and legal considerations are discussed before anything moves forward.

Boundaries

  • Artificial insemination only. No sexual contact.
  • Privacy and discretion are expected.
  • A legal agreement is strongly preferred before proceeding.
  • No co-parenting role unless explicitly discussed and agreed in writing.
  • Respectful communication.
