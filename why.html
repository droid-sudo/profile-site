Why I Am Open to Being a Known Donor

I am open to being a known donor because I understand that building a family can be a meaningful but difficult journey for many people. If my background, health information, and willingness to help can give intended parents another possible path, I would be happy to offer that support in a responsible and transparent way. I'm also very willing to support LGBTQ+ community.

My intention is to be a donor only. I do not seek parental rights, custody, decision-making authority, or a parenting role. I believe the child should be raised by the intended parent or parents, and I respect their family structure, values, and privacy.

I hope this profile gives a clear and respectful picture of who I am. My goal is simply to help, while keeping expectations, legal boundaries, and responsibilities clearly understood by everyone involved.
